Students Life & Activities

Bringing together diverse minds and different cultural perspectives, you’ll make friends and create
professional networks across a range of fields that will last throughout your lifetime and career.

Explore Our Campus,
Anytime, Anywhere

Student life at ILU is robust and full of new experiences. Our coursework not only includes class and lab times, but also site visits, interviews with experts, and both local and international travel.

Days at ILU incorporate practicing diplomatic skills in the Student Council, sharing meals in our award-winning canteen, and joining after school clubs in everything from football to cinema to foreign language acquisition.

Campus Highlights

The vibe at ILU is both fun and focused. You realize the people you meet in your programme, on campus, and at major university events will be future world-class game-changers—as will you—and that is inspiring!

The interactive community at ILU helps foster a healthy network of colleagues that can follow students for the rest of their lives, helping guarantee their long-term success.

Student Assembly Council

The Student Assembly Council is dedicated to fostering a vibrant and positive university environment at ILU. Our mission is to enhance the quality of student life, strengthen connections among ILU’s undergraduate community, and build meaningful relationships with the outside world, creating opportunities for mutual growth and uplifting our broader community.
