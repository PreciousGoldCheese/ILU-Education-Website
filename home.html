Leading the Future of Education

International Leadership University (ILU) is the leading private educational institution located in the heart of Myanmar’s capital city, Nay Pyi Taw. Among private universities in Myanmar, ILU is unique in both the breadth and depth of its critically acclaimed G2G accredited degree programmes which are available to both the general public and government employees.

Explore Our Academic Programs

ILU is home to programmes that help create a fairer, more equal world in the face of global challenges, particularly in the fields of sustainable economic development, policy analysis, transformative technology, public health, justice, and cultural understanding.

We’re excited for you to join us in translating world-class studies and research into real-world impact.

Life at ILU: Explore, Learn, Grow






Student life at ILU is robust and full of new experiences.Our coursework not only includes class and lab times,
but also site-visits, interviews with experts, and both local and international travel.

Days at ILU incorporate practicing diplomatic skills in the Student Council, sharing meals in our award-winning
canteen, and joining after school clubs in everything from football to cinema to foreign language acquisition.

The interactive community at ILU helps foster a healthy network of colleagues that can follow students for the rest of their lives, helping guarantee their long-term success.
