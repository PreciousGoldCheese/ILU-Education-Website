Admission to to ILU



We strive to admit the academically brightest students to ILU, regardless of background—people who demonstrate enthusiasm and passion about learning, and who plan to make a positive contribution to society. In fact, ILU was founded to bring international quality education to Myanmar especially for those who may have been excluded from it. That commitment remains as strong today as it was when we began.

Our equality, diversity and inclusion policy underlies every aspect of our student selection process. We want to admit excellent students who are likely to complete their degree programmes successfully and derive benefit from them, regardless of age, disability, ethnic origin, gender, religion, or political beliefs. We positively encourage and welcome applications all members of Myanmar society at ILU.

ILU’s access and participation plan outlines our commitment to widening participation at ILU.
