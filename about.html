Let Us Introduce Ourselves

We are International Leadership University (ILU), a leading private educational institution located in the heart of Myanmar’s capital city, Naypyitaw.

We offer a wide range of G2G accredited degree programs, making us unique among private universities in Myanmar, and our programs are available to both the general public and government employees.

ILU is the choice for Myanmar citizens planning to improve their lives and improve their country.

Welcome to International Leadership University, a place where we believe education is the cornerstone of societal progress.  At ILU, our vision is clear:  to think globally, but act locally, ensuring that our work resonates deeply within our communities while preparing our students for the challenges of an interconnected world.

Myanmar is at a pivotal moment in its history, and strong, principled leadership is essential.  That is why ILU is committed to going big—expanding our programs, growing our new campus, and offering opportunities that empower students to lead with confidence and integrity.

Leadership, after all, is not about titles or positions—it is found in the actions you take every day to create a better future.  I invite you to join us on this journey of growth and transformation.

Warm regards,
Ms May Phu Pwint Soe
Chairman




Dear Friends,

Let me tell you something I believe deep in my heart:  the future of Myanmar is as bright as the young people who walk through the doors of ILU every day.

I know this because we do more than teach skills here.  We empower students to lead, to stand tall, to honor the rich cultures that make our nation so very special. 

You know, one cannot just copy the way someone else built their life, business, or country.  Success doesn’t work that way.  Sustainable success is always built afresh, from the deepest roots of one’s own culture.

Accreditations and
Partnerships

ILU maintains a strong network of professional connections to assure its students receive only the best education possible.
