Creating A Campus For The Future

Building on our already progressive history, positive impact, and creative spirit, ILU is now in the process of creating an entirely new experiential and accessible learning environment in which transformative research, collaboration and engagement will create a better world. We call it the Eco-Tech Campus.

Our all new Eco-Tech Campus will be one-of-a-kind, expected to be the largest modern purpose-built private university or college campus in Myanmar.  Wander through its landscaped parks, eat at its massive international canteen, and explore the eco-friendly high-tech learning facilities perfectly built for cross-disciplinary research and open innovation.

This world-class academic campus boasts media labs, video editing studios, theatres, robotics labs, cyber-centres, language centres, classrooms, and the largest online-offline library in Myanmar.

Whatever you choose to study at ILU, the combinations and potential for discovery in our new space will be boundless.
